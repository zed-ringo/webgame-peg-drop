# Peg Drop Game Rules

Last updated: 2026-05-06

## Goal

Drop balls down a field of pegs and accumulate the highest score from where each ball lands.

## Rules

- A triangular field of pegs sits between a top drop area and a row of scoring slots at the bottom.
- The player chooses where (along the top) to release each ball.
- The ball falls under gravity, bouncing off pegs with slight randomness, and lands in one of the slots.
- Each slot has a fixed score value; landing in a slot adds that value.
- The run uses a fixed drop budget per difficulty.

## Controls

- Drag or move the pointer along the top to choose the drop position.
- Tap or click to drop the ball.
- Difficulty buttons restart with new layouts and budgets.

## Win / Lose

- Win: there is no fixed win; score is total accumulated across the budget.
- Lose: not used; best total score is saved per difficulty.
